# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in zib a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
background or identity, and to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, and healthy
community.

## Standards

Positive behavior includes empathy and kindness, respecting differing opinions, giving and
gracefully accepting constructive feedback, and taking responsibility for mistakes.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political
attacks, publishing others' private information, and other conduct reasonably considered
inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the maintainers via GitHub (open a
private report through the **Security** tab, or contact a maintainer directly). All complaints will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
